Nike produces a wide range of sports equipment and apparel. Their first products were track running shoes. Nike Air Max is a line of shoes first released by Nike, Inc. in 1987.
Additional product lines were introduced later, such as Air Huarache, which debuted in 1992. The most recent additions to their line are the Nike 6.0, Nike NYX, and Nike SB shoes, designed for skateboarding.
Nike has recently introduced cricket shoes called Air Zoom Yorker, designed to be 30% lighter than their competitors'.
In 2008, Nike introduced the Air Jordan XX3, a high-performance basketball shoe designed with the environment in mind.
